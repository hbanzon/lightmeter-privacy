Privacy Policy for LightMeter

Last updated: March 31, 2026

LightMeter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Camera Access: The app uses your device's camera solely to measure ambient light levels for exposure calculations. No photos or video are captured, saved, or transmitted.

No Analytics: The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

No Accounts: The app requires no account creation or sign-in.

All app data (ISO, shutter, and aperture preferences) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and never leaves your device.
